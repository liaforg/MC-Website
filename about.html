MARIE-CLAIRE SPIZZIRRI

Pro Makeup Artist | Makeup Instructor at Eden College


Toronto based makeup artist Marie-Claire Spizzirri learned at an early age the importance of loving what you do in life. Her mantra "do what you love and love what you do" has led her to surpass just being a makeup artist and become a master of her craft. Marie-Claire is able to attain her clients' vision by using high quality products and utilizing her many years of experience in bridal, fashion and creative photoshoots.

Marie-Claire graduated from Glow Academy in 2010 and has been following her passion ever since. Marie-Claire didn't stop there! She committed to furthering her skills in makeup by completing the Mario Dedivanovic (Kim Kardashian's makeup artist) workshop and learning from the best. Her expertise in the field has been featured in WedLuxe Bridal Magazine, as well as several movies and photoshoots. Her services include makeup lessons and makeup for all types of events including weddings, proms, corporate events and more. Marie-Claire has a full range of products and skill to suit any occasion, ethnicity and skin type.

Marie-Claire is a highly sought-after artist for her attention to detail, her professionalism and her knowledge as an industry expert.
